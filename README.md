<h1>SECTION 1 — INTRODUCTION</h1>

<p class=MsoNormal>This handbook sets out the terms and conditions of service,
benefits, and related employment matters for all employees (Executive and
Non‑Executive). It supplements individual contracts of service and supersedes
earlier handbooks of similar scope.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal>Management may update, revise, or add terms, conditions, or
appendices from time to time. Such amendments will be communicated via official
Human Resource circulars, which then form part of this handbook. Where
interpretation is unclear, employees should consult the Human Resource
Department. The contents are strictly confidential and intended solely for
internal reference.</p>

<h1>SECTION 2 — HUMAN RESOURCE POLICY</h1>

<p class=MsoNormal>The organization regards employees who collaborate to
achieve organizational objectives as key assets and representatives whose
strength drives growth and success. In this spirit, the organization aims to:</p>

<p class=MsoListBulletCxSpFirst><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>Provide competitive compensation and benefits consistent with
prevailing industry standards and trends.</p>

<p class=MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>Maintain a healthy, harmonious, and conducive work environment.</p>

<p class=MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>Engage personnel based on qualifications and experience.</p>

<p class=MsoListBulletCxSpLast><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>Ensure effective implementation of human resources policies.</p>

<h1>SECTION 3 — DEFINITIONS</h1>

<p class=MsoNormal>3.1 Employee Categories</p>

<p class=MsoListBulletCxSpFirst><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>Executive: Employees in designated Executive salary grades (e.g.,
EG8–EG18) on regular, permanent employment.</p>

<p class=MsoListBulletCxSpLast><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>Non‑Executive: Employees in designated Non‑Executive grades (see
Appendix I for job grade examples).</p>

<p class=MsoNormal>3.2 Immediate Family Members</p>

<p class=MsoListBulletCxSpFirst><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>Includes legal spouse, children, parents, parents‑in‑law,
brothers and sisters, grandparents, and legal guardians.</p>

<p class=MsoListBulletCxSpLast><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>In cases of more than one legal wife, only one wife should be
registered (Executive) / the first wife will be included (Non‑Executive).</p>

<p class=MsoNormal>3.3 Dependent Children</p>

<p class=MsoListBullet><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>Children up to 18 years old, or up to 23 if studying. Exceptions
apply where children are medically classified as disabled (mentally or
physically) and unable to support themselves.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal>3.4 Gender and Number</p>

<p class=MsoListBullet><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>Words in the masculine include the feminine; singular includes
plural and vice versa.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal>3.5 Day</p>

<p class=MsoListBullet><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>A continuous 24‑hour period from midnight to midnight, or any
24‑hour span measured from a given time point.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal>3.6 Leave Without Pay</p>

<p class=MsoListBullet><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>Leave taken without compensation.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal>3.7 Misconduct</p>

<p class=MsoListBullet><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>Any act or omission inconsistent with fulfilling expressed or
implied conditions of the contract of service.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal>3.8 Inefficiency</p>

<p class=MsoListBullet><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>Performance below organizational requirements (e.g., “Below
Requirements” / “Far Below Requirements”) repeated over time.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal>3.9 Employer</p>

<p class=MsoListBullet><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>Any party entering into a contract of service to employ another,
including agents, managers, or factors acting for that party.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal>3.10 Contract of Service</p>

<p class=MsoListBullet><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>Any agreement (oral/written; expressed/implied) under which one
party employs another and the other agrees to serve.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal>3.11 Panel Doctor</p>

<p class=MsoListBullet><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>Medical clinics/hospitals registered to provide medical attention
and treatment.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal>3.12 Registered Medical Practitioner / Medical Officer</p>

<p class=MsoListBullet><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>A practitioner registered under applicable medical laws (e.g.,
Medical Act 1971).</p>

<p class=MsoNormal>3.13 Shift Work</p>

<p class=MsoListBullet><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>Work requiring continuous or continual operation by two or more
shifts.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal>3.14 Public Statement</p>

<p class=MsoListBullet><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>Statements or comments made to press or public, in
lectures/speeches, or via sound/vision broadcasts.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal>3.15 Base Station</p>

<p class=MsoListBullet><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>The normal workplace where an employee is permanently assigned
and stationed.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal>3.16 Host Station</p>

<p class=MsoListBullet><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span>A workplace where an employee is assigned and stationed for
project or temporary purposes.</p>

<h1>SECTION 4 — CONDITIONS OF SERVICE</h1>

<p class=MsoNormal>4.1 Working Hours</p>

<p class=MsoListBulletCxSpFirst><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;

<p class=MsoListBulletCxSpLast><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span

<p class=MsoListBulletCxSpLast><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span

</span></span>Executive: Up to 6 months; probationary performance reports at

<p class=MsoListBulletCxSpLast><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;

<p class=MsoNormal>4.4 Confirmation</p>

<p class=MsoListBullet><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;

<p class=MsoListBulletCxSpFirst><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span

<p class=MsoListBulletCxSpLast><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span

<p class=MsoListBullet><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span

<p class=MsoListBulletCxSpFirst><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span

<p class=MsoListBulletCxSpLast><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span

<p class=MsoListBullet><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span

</span></span>24‑hour, 7‑day on‑call rotations for operations/maintenance

<p class=MsoNormal>4.15 Transferability</p>

<p class=MsoListBullet><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span

</span></span>Employees may be moved between departments/locations and assigned

<h1>SECTION 6 — LEAVE ENTITLEMENT</h1>

<p class=MsoListBullet><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span

</span></span>Office staff: Sunday. Operations/Maintenance (incl. Security for

<p class=MsoNormal>6.2 Public Holidays</p>

<p class=MsoListBulletCxSpFirst><span style='font-family:Symbol'>·<span
